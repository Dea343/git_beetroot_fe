Iron Man is the superhero alter ego of Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who creates a high-tech suit of armor to escape captivity and fight crime and terrorism. The character, co-created by Stan Lee and others, first appeared in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The suit grants him powers like flight, energy blasts, and enhanced strength, and in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), Robert Downey Jr. portrays the character, with the first movie released in 2008.
